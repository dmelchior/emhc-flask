How to Calculate Yardage

Multiply the three dimensions of your load together (length, width, and height) to find the number of cubic feet (0.5' x 12' x 12 = 72 cubic feet).
Divide the cubic feet by 27 (the number of cubic feet in a cubic yard) to find the number of cubic yards (72 ÷ 27 = 2.67 cu. yd.).
